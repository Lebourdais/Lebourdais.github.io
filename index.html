About Me

Hey,
I'm a new PhD graduate from Le Mans Université (France).
My current research topics are centred around overlapped speech. Either from a low-level perspective with the detection of overlapped speech from audio signal, or the detection of speech interruptions, that are correlated with overlapped areas.
I'm mainly focused on the audio modality with interests spanning from corpus creation to the evaluation of systems, analysis of results, and definition of evaluation frameworks.
